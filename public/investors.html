FAQ

Common Questions

When do I get paid? +
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid within 5 business days of month end. You receive a payment summary showing active referred customers and total commission earned.
Is there a minimum number of referrals? +
No minimum. Refer one customer, earn on one. Refer one hundred, earn on one hundred. There is no threshold, no activation requirement, no waiting period.
What happens after 12 months? +
The 12-month commission window runs from each referred customer's first payment date. After 12 months on that customer, commission ends. New customers you refer after that point each start a fresh 12-month window.
Can I refer institutions, not just individuals? +
Absolutely — institutional referrals produce the biggest commissions. A university on the $1,999 per month enterprise plan earns you $499.75 per month for 12 months — that is $5,997 total from a single referral.
How is my referral tracked? +
You receive a unique referral link with a tracking code. When someone visits via your link and subscribes within 90 days, that customer is attributed to you for the full 12-month commission window.
Is there a cap on total earnings? +
No cap whatsoever. If you refer $1M in ARR, you earn $250K. The program is designed without a ceiling to reward high-impact partners at any scale.
