FAQ

Common Questions

What is the user referral commission rate? +
20% of each referred user's monthly subscription, paid monthly for 12 months from their first payment date. If you refer someone on the Scholar plan ($9/mo), you earn $1.80/month for 12 months. No minimum, no threshold.
How does the investor referral commission work? +
If you introduce an investor who participates in our $2M seed round, you earn 2% of their investment amount. This is paid from SeenShown's own fund allocation — the investor receives their full investment position with no deduction. Example: refer a $500K investor, earn $10,000, paid within 30 days of close.
When do I get paid? +
User referral commissions are calculated monthly and paid within 5 business days of month end. Investor referral commissions are paid as a lump sum within 30 days of the investment close date.
Is there a cap on earnings? +
No cap on user referral commissions. Investor referral commissions are naturally bounded by the $2M round size — maximum 2% of $2M if you referred the entire round, which would be $40,000.
How is my referral tracked? +
You receive a unique referral link. Users who subscribe within 90 days of clicking your link are attributed to you for the full 12-month window. Investor referrals are tracked by direct introduction — you introduce us via email and we confirm attribution before close.
What happens after 12 months for user referrals? +
The 12-month window runs from each user's first payment. After 12 months on that user, commission ends. New users you refer after that point each start a fresh 12-month window.
